Possible Pitfalls

Bar Chart

Categorical x-axis

⚠ The x-axis categories may have an inherit ordering. Are they ordered correctly?

Numeric x-axis

⚠ If the x-axis is numeric, what categories are being used? Does it make sense to categorize this data?

Categorical y-axis

⚠ Categorical independent axes are rare- does it make sense for this type of data?

Numeric y-axis

⚠ Does the y-axis start at zero? (Or have a clear zero point if it includes negative values?

  • Bar charts not starting from zero tend to distort the scale of differences in data.

⚠ The axis is measuring percentages- is it the percentage of a whole, or the percentage changed?

Bar with Line

⚠ Is it clear what the line is measuring?

⚠ Does the line use the same scale and axis as the bars?

⚠ Lines imply continuity between categories, are these categories continuous?

Line Chart

⚠ Lines imply continuity between data points- is the data continuous?

Categorical x-axis

⚠ The x-axis categories may have an inherit ordering. Are they ordered correctly?

Numeric x-axis

⚠ If the x-axis is numeric, what categories are being used? Does it make sense to categorize this data?

Categorical y-axis

⚠ Categorical independent axes are rare- does it make sense for this type of data?

Numeric y-axis

⚠ Does the y-axis start at zero? (Or have a clear zero point if it includes negative values?

  • Bar charts not starting from zero tend to distort the differences in data.

⚠ Is the range of the axis appropriately sized for the data?

  • Using too large of a range can minimize the appearance of differences between data points.
  • Depending on what the axis represents, too small of a range may overstate differences.

⚠ Is the axis ordered in the appropriate direction? In general, zero should be on the bottom.

⚠ The axis is measuring percentages- is it the percentage of a whole, or the percentage changed?

General Considerations

⚠ Does the chart tell where the data came from? Is it a reputable source?

⚠ If certain points are highlighted or called out, why were these chosen? Do they draw attention away from general trends in the data?

⚠ How are colors used in this chart?

  • Do they draw attention to or from certain points or categories? If so, why?
  • Is it easy to distinguish between colors that represent different things?
